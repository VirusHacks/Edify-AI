## 1️⃣ THE PROBLEM (2 minutes)

### Open with Relatable Pain
> "How many of you have applied to 50+ jobs and heard nothing back?"
> 
> "How many have spent hours tailoring a resume, only to get rejected by an algorithm you never even met?"
>
> "How many have walked into an interview and wished you'd practiced more - but didn't know HOW to practice alone?"

### The Statistics
- **75% of resumes** are rejected by ATS before a human sees them
- Average job seeker spends **11 hours per application** (research + tailoring + prep)
- **85% of job seekers** feel unprepared for interviews
- Career skills become outdated in **2-3 years** - courses can't keep up

### The Real Problem
> "The career preparation industry is broken. It's fragmented across 10 different tools that don't talk to each other. You use Jobscan for ATS, Coursera for learning, LinkedIn for jobs, Pramp for interviews - and YOU are the one manually connecting all the dots."

1. "Let me upload a resume and paste a job description"
2. "Watch - 5 specialized AI agents analyze in parallel"
3. "Skills Agent: 72/100 - missing Python, AWS"
4. "Experience Agent: 85/100 - good relevant experience"
5. "See the weighted breakdown - this is how recruiters actually think"
6. **Click: "Generate Roadmap for Missing Skills"**

**Part B: Personalized Roadmap (60 seconds)**
1. "Notice I didn't re-enter anything - the skill gaps flowed automatically"
2. "This roadmap is generated from LIVE job market data"
3. "See the citations? These are real job postings from this week"
4. "Salary data: $95K entry, $140K senior - current market rates"

**Part C: Mock Interview (90 seconds)**
1. "Now let's practice - questions are generated from MY resume"
2. "Watch the eye contact indicator - green means I'm looking at camera"
3. *Answer one question while looking away*
4. "See? It caught that. This is real-time body language analysis"
5. "Submit - instant rating: 7/10, plus specific feedback"

## ❌ Things to AVOID

1. **Don't say "AI-powered"** - Everyone says this. Say what the AI actually DOES.
2. **Don't compare to ChatGPT** - We're architecturally different, not a wrapper.
3. **Don't oversell** - Stick to features you can demo.
4. **Don't get lost in technical details** - Lead with user value, technical is backup.
5. **Don't end weakly** - Have a prepared, powerful closing line.
